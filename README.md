# Building-Belonging-HR-Innovation-for-Securtech-S.p.A.

**Challenge:** KPMG · Università di Bologna · Magistrale  
**Team:** 7 membri

---

## Overview

The project addresses a recurring challenge in structured organizations:
how to sustain employee motivation, recognition, and internal cohesion —
especially in high-pressure environments.

Working with the fictional case of Securtech S.p.A., the team designed
four integrated initiatives targeting two core dimensions: motivation
and team cohesion.

---

## Initiatives

### LinkMeUp — Real-Time Support
A real-time availability signaling system embedded in the company
management platform. When a team member completes a task, they can
broadcast their availability to colleagues, who can accept the request
on a first-come basis. The system automatically coordinates the
collaboration — assigning a virtual room for remote workers or a
meeting point for those in-office.

**Goal:** reduce internal pressure in high-workload situations by
stimulating peer-to-peer support.

**KPIs:**
- `SendReceive` — ratio of accepted help requests over total sent,
  tracked monthly
- `TeamPressureIndex (TPI)` — composite index based on overtime hours,
  workload, and task error rate; thresholds at 30% (low) and 60% (high)

---

### Q-izzati — Monthly Knowledge Quiz
A monthly multiple-choice quiz tailored to each employee's professional
domain. Scores are private and tracked over time in a personal
dashboard. Employees scoring below the minimum threshold (60 pts)
receive targeted learning suggestions. A semi-annual bonus is awarded
to the top performer.

**Goal:** foster continuous self-development and professional growth
through structured, recurring self-assessment.

**KPI:** `GrDe` — semi-annual sum of monthly scores per employee
(threshold: 360 pts over 6 months)

---

### SecureBoard — Virtual Recognition Board
An internal social board integrated into the company platform, where
lead management and team leads can publish relevant milestones — project
results, external recognitions, publications. Employees can interact
through likes and comments.

Access is role-based: publishing rights are reserved for management;
all employees can view. This structure preserves the perceived value of
recognition while enabling both top-down and peer recognition dynamics.

**Goal:** increase visibility of individual and team achievements,
reinforcing a culture of recognition.

---

### BattleZone — Cross-Team Challenge
A 6-month structured competition between stable teams, judged by client
satisfaction scores collected through standardized Likert-scale
questionnaires at the end of each project. The ranking is based on the
average satisfaction score per team across all clients served during
the period.

**Goal:** foster internal cohesion through a shared objective and
healthy competitive spirit.

**Prizes:** company-sponsored team trip · recognition post on SecureBoard

---

## Framework

| Initiative | Dimension | Mechanism |
|---|---|---|
| LinkMeUp | Cohesion | Peer support under pressure |
| Q-izzati | Motivation | Self-assessment and growth |
| SecureBoard | Motivation | Recognition and visibility |
| BattleZone | Cohesion | Shared goal and competition |
